Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ludo Fire is developed by Adroja Infotech.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information from users.

However, the app may collect limited information automatically for functionality and advertising purposes, including:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID (used by Google AdMob for showing ads)

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Display personalized or non-personalized advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

Ludo Fire uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google Play Services

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here.

4. Children’s Privacy

Ludo Fire does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not host user data. For any data collected by third-party SDKs, users can manage their privacy via device settings (Reset Advertising ID). For any queries, contact us at the email below.
